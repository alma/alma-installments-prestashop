In Page documentation
===================================

## Context
The In Page option allows you to avoid the redirection to the Alma Checkout and keep the customer on the merchant's website during the entire checkout process.
This option is enabled by default, but you can disable it if you want to redirect the customer to the Alma Checkout.
In the configuration page you can also custom the selector of the button `Place order button selector` that will trigger the In Page checkout, and the selector of input payment button `Input payment button Alma selector` that will trigger the In Page.
